About Andrew

Andrew was born in Decatur, GA and currently resides in Cumming, GA. After receiving a BS in Exercise Science from Georgia College and an MS in Exercise Physiology from the University of Miami, Andrew has spent the last 10 years working as a strength coach, personal trainer, and group exercise instructor.

Ever ambitious, Andrew is currently attending a coding boot camp from the Georgia Institute of Technology to become a software developer and merge his passions of strength and conditioning and fitness with the high-paced world of technology.
